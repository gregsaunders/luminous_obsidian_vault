## üéØ Campaign Philosophy

**You are the bridge between cutting-edge research and business reality.**

Researchers have the science. Operators need practical solutions. You translate between these worlds, providing:
- Business perspective on scientific breakthroughs
- Operational feasibility insights
- Cost-effectiveness analysis
- Regulatory pathway clarity
- Technology integration vision

**Your unique value:** You understand both the biology (biosensors) and the business (Luminous Bio Solutions), giving you credibility to comment from a commercial lens while respecting the science.

**Tone:** Optimistic thought leader - enthusiastic about breakthroughs while acknowledging challenges. Forward-thinking but credible. Inviting dialogue, not preaching solutions.
