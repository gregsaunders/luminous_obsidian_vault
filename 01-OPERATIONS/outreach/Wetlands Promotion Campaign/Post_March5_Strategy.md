## üéì Workshop Development Strategy

### Workshop Concept 1: "Understanding Biosensor Technology for Environmental Monitoring"
**Target Audience:** Lab managers, environmental consultants, site managers
**Duration:** 2 hours (virtual or in-person)
**Content:**
- How biosensors work (biology fundamentals)
- Luminous Bio Solutions' specific NA biosensors
- Validation data and accuracy
- Field deployment protocols
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Q&A and discussion

**Pricing:** $199/person (virtual), $399/person (in-person)
**Goal:** 20-30 attendees for pilot workshop

---

### Workshop Concept 2: "Implementing Wetland Monitoring Programs"
**Target Audience:** Oil sands operators, remediation project managers
**Duration:** Half-day (4 hours)
**Content:**
- Monitoring objectives for wetland systems
- Regulatory requirements and compliance
- Sampling protocols and frequency
- Data management and interpretation
- Integration of biosensors with traditional methods
- Case studies and lessons learned

**Pricing:** $499/person (in-person preferred, Calgary or Fort McMurray)
**Goal:** 15-25 attendees, potential for multiple sessions

---

### Workshop Concept 3: "Business Case for Nature-Based Tailings Treatment"
**Target Audience:** C-suite, sustainability VPs, financial decision-makers
**Duration:** 2-3 hours (executive briefing format)
**Content:**
- Scale of tailings challenge and closure obligations
- Overview of wetland treatment approach (high-level)
- Economic comparison: CAPEX/OPEX vs conventional treatment
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Regulatory pathway and timeline
- Social licence and ESG benefits
- ROI modeling

**Pricing:** $799/person (small group, high-value audience)
**Goal:** 10-15 senior decision-makers
