### [2025-11-22] Learned Today: Dave Lee's "Make It Easy" Sales Philosophy

**What we learned:**
Operators will dictate unfavorable terms or slow-roll discussions if you don't come with specific financial and operational metrics. The solution: "Make it as easy as you can for them to take the medicine they need."

**What "Make It Easy" Means:**
1. **Specific pricing** with clear ROI math (not "let's discuss")
2. **Minimal work on their side** (zero-friction deployment)
3. **Pre-answered objections** (risk reversal, data ownership)
4. **Tangible deliverables** (not "monitoring service" but "4 asset categories")

**Applied to Pilot Proposal:**

- 50% grant matching (CNRL pays half)

### [2025-11-22] Learned Today: Deliverables = Lasting Assets (Not Just Services)

**What we learned:**
Framing pilot outputs as permanent operational assets (not "9 months of monitoring") creates:
1. **Internal budget justification** - "Here's what we get for the money"
2. **Stickiness** - Switching to competitor means starting over
3. **Expansion lever** - "Imagine this at all your sites"
4. **Multi-department value** - Ops, Environment, Finance, Regulatory all see something

**Four Deliverable Categories:**
1. **Readiness Baseline Report** (Regulatory Asset) - 2027 release documentation
2. **Operational Intelligence Package** (Efficiency Asset) - Site-specific insights
3. **ROI Validation Report** (Business Case Asset) - Quantified savings, expansion projections
4. **Future Project Intelligence** (Strategic Asset) - Treatment recommendations, monitoring optimization

**The Lasting Value Statement:**
"These deliverables don't expire when the pilot ends. They become your baseline for all future water release applications, your playbook for optimizing operations across all sites, and your competitive advantage over operators who started later."
