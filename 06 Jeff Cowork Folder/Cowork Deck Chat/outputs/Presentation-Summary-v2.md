### SLIDE 1: Title Slide

### SLIDE 2: The Problem - Massive Scale
**Purpose**: Establish enormous scale with ARO context  
**Key Visual**: Large statistics, amber accent bar, blue ARO context box  
**Duration**: 90 seconds

**Content:**
- **1.4 Trillion litres** of tailings water (560,000 Olympic pools, size of Calgary)
- 60+ years of "Zero Discharge" policy
- NAs are the primary blocker preventing regulatory approval
- **NEW**: Blue box highlighting "These obligations sit on balance sheets TODAY"

**Key Message**: This isn't an oversight - it was deliberate policy. And these liabilities exist on balance sheets right now.

---

### SLIDE 3: The Hidden Balance Sheet Opportunity (NEW)
**Purpose**: Frame ARO as opportunity through monitoring certainty  
**Key Visual**: Blue gradient header, three operator boxes, amber insight box  
**Duration**: 2 minutes

**Content:**
- **Combined ARO: $20+ Billion** across CNRL, Suncor, Imperial
- **Operator Breakdown**:
  - CNRL: $1.9B (Oil Sands Mining & Upgrading)
  - Suncor: $12.3B (Total Decommissioning)
  - Imperial: $2.6B (ARO & Environmental)
- **Key Insight**: "Monitoring certainty reduces conservative assumptions"
  - 60-year discounting makes small improvements = big balance sheet impact
  - Better data justifies less conservative rates and timelines

**Key Message**: Not as bad as you think—better monitoring gives confidence to improve financial statements.

---

### SLIDE 4: Crisis Converging - Why Now?
**Purpose**: Show multiple simultaneous pressures forcing change  
**Key Visual**: Four blue pressure boxes (Economic, Physical, Regulatory, Social), amber warning box  
**Duration**: 90 seconds

**Content:**
- **Economic**: Double production requires more water, NAs prevent it
- **Physical**: 1.4T litres approaching capacity
- **Regulatory**: Release standards expected 2026-27
- **Social License**: Indigenous demand: "If you drink it, we'll consider release"

**Key Message**: Status quo (indefinite containment) is no longer viable.

---

### SLIDE 5: The Monitoring Gap
**Purpose**: Identify the specific technical blocker  
**Key Visual**: Two red comparison boxes (FTIR vs HRMS), amber impact box  
**NEW Tagline**: "You can't regulate what you can't measure"  
**Duration**: 90 seconds

**Content:**
- Treatment technologies **exist** - but operators are flying blind
- **FTIR**: Fast (minutes) but inaccurate (false positives/negatives)
- **HRMS**: Accurate but slow (4-8 weeks, $500-1000/sample)
- **Real example**: Wetland study - 9 samples over 2 seasons, degradation decline detected 6 months too late

**Key Message**: The test that's fast enough isn't accurate enough. The test that's accurate enough isn't fast enough.

---

### SLIDE 6: The Solution - Bridge the Monitoring Gap
**Purpose**: Present Luminous' three-tier integration + three-pronged value  
**Key Visual**: Three colored tier boxes (green, blue, purple), green validation box, amber three-pronged value box  
**Duration**: 2 minutes

**Content:**
**Three-Tier Integration:**
- **Tier 1: Compliance** - HRMS (quarterly, regulatory, forensic)
- **Tier 2: Operations** - Luminous Biosensor (24-72hr, daily/weekly, bioavailable NAs)
- **Tier 3: Intelligence** - Confluent Platform (real-time, dashboards, audit trail)

**Validation:**
- Published in ACS Synthetic Biology (2024)
- Field-tested at Kearl wetland with high correlation to Orbitrap Mass Spec
- Accepted to Creative Destruction Lab Energy Stream (2025)

**Three-Pronged Value Chain:**
1. **ARO Impact**: 6 months = $45M. 1% certainty = $19M. More usage = better certainty = greater reduction.
2. **Operational**: Real-time optimization, extended seasons, faster detection ($260k/year validated)
3. **Social License**: Transparent data for all stakeholders builds trust

**Key Message**: Not replacement - integration. Making HRMS more valuable with operational context.

## Usage Recommendations

**For Investor Pitch:**
- Emphasize CDL acceptance, peer-review, validated value
- Focus on market need (all operators need this), regulatory tailwind
- Highlight ARO impact as significant additional value beyond operational savings
- Three-pronged value de-risks the business case
- This is infrastructure play - recurring revenue model

**For Operator Outreach:**
- Lead with operational pain points (flying blind, 6-month delayed feedback)
- ARO reduction potential as major balance sheet opportunity
- Emphasize risk reduction and faster optimization
- Frame as compliance readiness advantage (12-18 month head start)
- More usage = better certainty = greater ARO reduction (compounding benefits)

**For Stakeholder/Public:**
- Lead with transparency and accountability
- Emphasize community access to same data as operators
- Focus on environmental outcomes (safe water release, land restoration)
- ARO framing shows this is economically viable, not just environmental cost
